About Us

ABA's UAV Remote Ground Station: Embracing the Future of UAVs

Our project focuses on the exciting realm of UAV remote operations via air-link, a rapidly evolving technology that has vast potential in numerous applications.

We aim to construct a UAV and develop an online remote station capable of controlling the UAV from any location with an internet connection. This comprehensive study will contribute to advancing UAV technology and its practical applications across various industries.

Welcome to ABA's UAV Remote Ground Station

Expertly designed and developed by Aba Sargious

This application offers:

  • Manual Control: Directly pilot the UAV.
  • Autonomous Flight: Configure your own flight path for autonomous operations.
  • Real-time dashboard: Monitor vital UAV telemetry data including GPS, velocity, and more.

We aim to push the boundaries of what is possible with UAV technology, exploring diverse applications including agricultural monitoring, construction site surveying, and critical area surveillance.
